Welcome to The Maritime Forum


WHO ARE WE


The MAritime Forum is a platform created to engage students who aspire to develop a career in the Maritime industry. This is achieved through organising seminars,skill aquisition,empowerment programmes,internships,scholarships,and technical activities.


ABOUT US

The Maritime Forum, University of Lagos is a student society established in 2015 by a commitee of seven(7) Law stdents who observed the dearth of interest in the Maritime Industry and felt the urgency to create awareness about the importance of the industry to economic development in Nigeria. The committee headed by MR. Balogun Adedeji the coordinator and funding father f the society. The society since ts establishment has carried out various enlightenment and educational programmes wthin and outside the unversity environment


OUR VISION STATEMENT

To place the Martime industry at the forefront and lead the revitalization of the Nigerian economy through education and exposure of the youths



OUR MISSION

To be the umbrella organisation of all students interested in the Maritime sector prviding a platform for youth led programs, a forum for dialogue and network of future experts in the maritime industry


CORE VALUES

  • iNTEGRITTY
  • LEADERSHIP
  • INNOVATION AND
  • PROFESSIONALISM

The Taiwo Afolabi Annual Maritime Conference (TAAM)

The Forum, has successfully organized the first,Second and third edition of the Taiwo Afolabi Annual Maritime Conference(TAAM) and Blueprint competition, the conference is a National conference organized manually by the Maritime Forum in patnership with SIFAX GROUP. The aim of the conference is to promote the knowledge of the Maritime Industry in Afrca. The Conference has in attendance distinguished personalities in te industry as Barrister Hassan Bello,CEO Nigerian Shippers Council,Princess Vick Haastrup,the Vice president ENL Consortium, Majr Henry Ajetunmobi, Mrs Bala Usman Hadiza,Managing Director, Nigerian Ports Authority, Dr Ay Jadesimi, The Managing Director,LADOL,Mr Jean Anishere amongst many other experenced personalites in the industry



A FIELD TRIP TO LADOL IN 2017

LADOL(lLagos Deep Offshore Logistic Base), officially known as LADOL Free Zone also known as LADOL Base or he initials LFZ, is an industrial Free Zone privately owned logistic and engineering facility located on an island in the port of Apapa, Lags, NIgeria. it is the first and only base specifically designed to meet the logistical needs of deep water offshore oil and gas operations and projects in and around Nigeria.
